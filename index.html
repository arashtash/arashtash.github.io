About me

My name is Arash and I'm a third-year Computer Science student at Dalhousie University. My journey with programming started with my brother teaching me Java in 2014. Since then, I have expanded my knowledge and skills to include various programming languages, tools and frameworks including Python, C, C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js and Spring Boot. I have also learned and gained hands-on experience with Object-Oriented Programming, teamwork, Git, and Test-Driven Development in my courses at Dalhousie University.

I became particularly enthusiastic about Software and Web Development after doing a part-time Web Development internship in the Summer of 2018. Ever since then, I have always been eager to learn about new technologies and trends and use them in the projects that I work on.

After starting my degree I became especially interested in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence and their significant and increasing impacts on our lives. As a result, I am planning to complete my degree with a certificate in Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Systems from Dalhousie University.

When not studying, I spend most of my free time working on software development projects, watching Udemy courses, playing the piano, singing, learning French, hanging out with friends and family, and playing video games.
